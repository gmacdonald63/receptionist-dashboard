How the AI Call Flow Works

1. Incoming Call

Customer calls your business number.

2. AI Answers

Friendly, professional HVAC-trained receptionist answers instantly. Or can be set to answer when you're not able to (instead of going to voicemail).

3. Details Collected

Problem, address, urgency, and availability captured.

4. Appointment Booked

Appointment appears in your dashboard instantly. If it's an emergency, the call gets transferred to you.
